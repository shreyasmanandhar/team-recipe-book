Delicious Classic Cheesecake

By: Shreyas

Ingredients:

  • 200g biscuits (crushed)
  • 100g butter (melted)
  • 600g cream cheese (softened)
  • 150g sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 200ml sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F) and grease a 23cm springform pan. We want a smooth, non-stick base.
  2. Mix the crushed biscuits with melted butter and press it firmly into the pan to form the base.
  3. In a bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth and creamy. There should be no lumps.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing gently after each. Treat it like a delicately.
  5. Fold in the sour cream until everything is beautifully combined.
  6. Pour this mixture over your biscuit base, spreading it evenly.
  7. Bake for 50–60 minutes. The center should jiggle slightly like a pudding.
  8. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.
  9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (or overnight) before slicing. Patience is the key in this recipe.

Cooking time: 1 hour 15 minutes + chilling

Serves: Generally 8 people (but depends on who's eating)
