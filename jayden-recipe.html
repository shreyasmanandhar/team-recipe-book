Homemade Hamburger Helper

By: Jayden

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 tbsp Conrstarch
  • 1 tbsp Conrstarch
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsps Garlic Powder
  • 1 1/2 tsps Onion Powder
  • 1 1/2 tsps Salt
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 cups Hot Water
  • 1 1/2 cups Milk
  • 2 cups Macaroni Noodles
  • 2 cups Cheddar Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Brown your 1 pound of ground beef in large skillet over medium-high heat. Break it up meet to make sure it cooks evenly and drain any extra fat.
  2. Sprinkle your spices and cornstarch over the meat and stir before adding hot water, milk and pasta.
  3. Add in your noodles to the mix.
  4. Bring to a boil, then cover with a lid and reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook over medium heat until pasta is tender.
  5. Once noodles have absorbed most of the liquids, sprinkle cheddar cheese over top, then stir it in.
  6. After a little longer of simmering, the extra liquids and cheese will create a thick sauce, which completes the meal.

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Serves: 6 people
